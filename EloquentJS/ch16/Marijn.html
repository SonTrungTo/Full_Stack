Marijn: A platform game

  • Press the cursor keys; ArrowUp, ArrowLeft, ArrowRight; to jump, move left and move right, respectively.
  • Press ESC to pause the game anytime.
  • Avoid being scorched and get all gold coins to win the game.
  • Be careful of monsters:they run fast, jump unpredictably and can be defeated if the player lands his/her feet on them.
