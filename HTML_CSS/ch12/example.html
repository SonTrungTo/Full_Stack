Microeconomics

a text most grad students hate!

Microeconomic Theory is a terrific text for first-year graduate student in Microeconomics. Its first draft was used to teach first-year graduate students at Harvard. The text includes all common topics a teacher would like to consider for teaching. The book contains sections on Game Theory, which essentially is covered from chapter 7 to chapter 9. Its game theory sections include standard topics: dominated strategy, dominant strategy, Nash Equlibrium, etc...
